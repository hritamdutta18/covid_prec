COVID-19 Precautions

  • Wash Hands

    Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

  • Social Distancing

    Maintain a safe distance from everyone even if they aren't coughing or sneezing.

  • Wear Mask

    Wear a mask irrespective of whether physical distancing is possible or not.

  • Cover while sneezing/coughing

    Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.

  • Stay Home

    Stay home if you feel unwell. Going out may spread the virus as you can infect others.

  • Seek Medical Help

    If you have a fever, cough or difficulty in breathing, seek medical attention. A right health facility protects you, and prevents the spread of viruses & other infections.
